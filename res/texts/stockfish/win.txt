This is the installation guide for Stockfish chess engine.
It is a very simple procedure with four simple steps.

Step 1: Head over to downloads page of stockfish,
          stockfishchess.org.

Step 2: Download the zipfile for Windows and extract
          it in any folder on your computer.

Step 3: In this Folder, you will see a folder named
          Windows. Inside which, there will be a set of
          stockfish executables. Choose the one that
          says 32-bit executable.

Step 4: Copy this file and paste in the following
          path after renaming the exe to stockfish.exe.
